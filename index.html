Brandon Woodward

My name is Brandon Woodward and I'm a Master's student at Carnegie Mellon University studying Artificial Intelligence Engineering - Mechanical Engineering. 

That's one major - not two - and it essentially means I'm studying AI and ML with it's applications to mechanical engineering. I've been interested in these applications since I studied Mechanical Engineering at Georgia Tech during my undergrad, where I minored in robotics and took several CS / AI classes. I'm focusing my master's studies on overlapping AI and controls. 

I'm a always eager learn and love looking for ways to apply the knowledge I've picked up in class in real life. Because of this I have done research in multiple labs as an undergraduate at GT and plan to continue doing research next semester at CMU.

I'm currently working on the 4NE-1 Humanoid team at Neura Robotics in Germany. Moving to Germany has been an amazing experience and I've been making the most of my time in Europe, and I love the work I'm doing at Neura robotics. 

I'm looking for a fulltime job starting in the summer of 2025. My career fields of interest are humanoid robotics and deep reinforcement learning. If you have any questions please reach out via the contact me page and I'll get back to you as soon as possible!
